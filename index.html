About

Hello, my name is Jay.
I'm South Korean, but I was born and raised here in Florida my entire life.

Throughout my life, I never really knew what I wanted to be, career-wise. I thought I'd become an artist because I love drawing, but I realized it's something I'd rather keep as a stress-free hobby (and I was always too squeamish to become a nurse like my parents wanted me to be).

But then, I stumbled upon data entry and analysis at my workplace. I realized I had to deal with and maintain a lot of data in what I do if I were to ever hope to be efficient at my job, I need to be able to manage it and manage it well.

So I started taking classes! And with a good mentor and an excellent environment for applying the knowledge, I started absorbing information and gaining new skills very quickly. As a problem-solver, I get excited when I think about how many issues, big and small, could be solved if we just know where to look. At my core, I've also always enjoyed contributing towards helping others, and so to find a field checks off both of those without all the blood and guts of nursing? What a find!

My journey with data has not been long, but it has been an exciting one. I may not have mastered my craft yet, but I'm proud of where I am now, and am excited to see where I'll be in the field a few years from now. I hope you'll join me as I grow overtime.
